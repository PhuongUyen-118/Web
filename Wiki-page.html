The Brown Bear

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is a large mammal found across North America, Europe, and Asia. It is known for its strength, adaptability, and diverse diet.

Learn more about the brown bear on Wikipedia.

Top 3 Characteristics

  1. Strong and powerful physique
  2. Omnivorous diet (eats berries, fish, and small mammals)
  3. Hibernates during winter months

Natural Habitats

  • Forests and woodlands
  • Mountains and tundras
  • River valleys and coastal regions
